About Us

GoIGNIS is a team of skilled and experienced engineers based out of India who specialize in providing top-quality design and engineering services to US-based MEP companies and consultants. Our team is well-versed with codes and standards including NFPA, FM, UL & local AHJ guidelines, and we offer a wide range of engineering services including mechanical, electrical, Fire Alarm & Protection, and ARC solutions.

Our Services

Fire Alarm Engineering

At GoIGNIS, we understand the critical role that a well-designed fire alarm system plays in protecting life and property. Our engineers have the expertise to design fire alarm systems that offer complete coverage as per NPFA & local AHJ requirements.

Electrical Engineering

Our electrical engineering team at GoIGNIS understands the ever-increasing complexity and functionality of building systems. We are committed to providing quality electrical designs that ensure projects are on time and within budget.

HVAC Engineering

Our team of experienced HVAC designers will work with your team to create a functional and efficient system to meet your needs.
